> Link Discord accounts to Roblox profiles so you can gate your community, confirm identities, and keep impersonators out.

The **Verification** module links Discord accounts to Roblox profiles so you can gate your community and confirm member identities.

## Commands

- **`/verify`** — Link and verify your Roblox account *(All members)*
- **`/unverify`** — Remove your verification *(All members)*
- **`/forceverify`** — Force-verify a user and apply roles *(Server Administrator)*

## How to configure

1. Run **`/config`** or **`w!config`**
2. Select **Verification**
3. Enable the module and assign verified and unverified roles
4. Choose a verification type under **Select a Verification Type...**

## Settings

| Setting | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Module State** | Enable or disable the module. |
| **Verified Role(s)** | Roles assigned after a member verifies. Up to 10 roles. |
| **Unverified Role(s)** | Roles assigned to members who have not verified yet. Up to 10 roles. |

## Verification types

| Type | Description |
| --- | --- |
| **Default Verification** | Members verify through a dedicated verification channel. Set the channel and post the verification message from the config flow. |
| **Verify On Join** | Members are prompted to verify automatically when they join the Discord server. |

<Note>
The verified nickname format defaults to `{roblox.username}`. Additional nickname and embed customization options may be available in future updates.
</Note>
