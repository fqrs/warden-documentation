Warden connects your Discord server to your ER:LC private server. Staff run moderation, sessions, and logs from Discord. Members verify, apply, and leave feedback without leaving the server.

<Card title="Invite Warden" icon="discord" href="https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1506630562113130657">
  Add Warden to your server, then open **`/config`** to turn on the modules you need.
</Card>
