Learn how to start using Warden to manage and transform your server!

**Warden** makes staff management _effortless_. Built for simplicity and ease of use, our tools help you configure and automate your server's daily operations in just a few _clicks_.

## What can you do with Warden?

- **Punish** players for breaking server rules.
- Track staff _performance_ and activity with our **Shift Logging.**
- Stay _informed_ with real-time tracking of **In-Game Logs** _such as: (command logs, join and leave logs, modcall logs, and kill logs)_.
- Manage your **Sessions** with ease.
- Manage Staff Infractions.
