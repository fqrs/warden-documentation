Warden is an all-in-one ER:LC bot designed to make managing your server easier, filled with a variety of tools from moderation to automation that will help your server to be managed easily and smoothly with: Roblox Verification & Moderation, Feedback, Sessions, Shifts, ER:LC Logs, Applications and more...

<Card title="Invite Warden" icon="discord" href="https://discord.com/oauth2/authorize?client_id=1506630562113130657">
  Add Warden to your server, then configure Warden over at [Dashboard](https://wrdnbot.xyz/dashboard)
</Card>
